How does this work?

Getting into the Kenzie FLOW is simple
1. Plan out what you want to finish
2. Start the FLOW timer and focus on that goal for 25 minutes
3. Take a short break once the 25 minutes is up
4. Iterate step 2 and 3 until you've completed 4 flows
5. Take a long break
